Who Am I

I'm Ussama Hussein — a creative entrepreneur, storyteller, and modern-day polymath. I blend art with ambition, driven by a passion for visual storytelling, AI innovation, and refined experiences. From cinematic videos to intelligent systems, from bold branding to business automation—I turn ideas into timeless, high-impact realities. I’m the founder and owner of Griffaire Group — a collective that brings together innovation, creativity, and culture under one vision. Within it, I lead: Maison Vérité – a marketing company for modern brands with timeless style Griffaire X – focused on software, AI, and digital innovation A learning platform for entrepreneurs and creators A full-scale production company, which I own fully Each brand is built in partnership with talented minds—except the production company, which is my personal passion project. Whether I’m directing a shoot, designing a brand, building tech, or shaping strategy, I move with the mindset of both a creator and a CEO. I draw inspiration from vintage grit, modern elegance, and the freedom of building your own legacy. This isn’t just business—it’s personal. It’s a lifestyle. And it’s built to last. Let’s create something remarkable.
